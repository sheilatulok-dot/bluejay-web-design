Our story

A city retreat with a warmer point of view.

Sapphira began as an idea for a spa that felt polished but never impersonal—an inviting space where considered design supports genuine comfort.

The updated concept balances organic colour, restrained materials, clear service information, and generous breathing room. The result is a brand experience that feels restorative before a treatment even begins.

About this portfolio project

Original concept, thoughtfully revisited.

Sapphira Spa & Salon is a fictional concept website originally created by Sheila Tulok for a college assignment. Sheila designed the Sapphira logo in Adobe Illustrator, using softly curved lettering, leaf-inspired geometric accents, and a warm natural palette to give the brand a welcoming character.

This updated portfolio edition demonstrates responsive Bootstrap development, content organization, accessible navigation, visual art direction, and a more polished presentation of the original idea. It does not represent a current client or operating spa.
