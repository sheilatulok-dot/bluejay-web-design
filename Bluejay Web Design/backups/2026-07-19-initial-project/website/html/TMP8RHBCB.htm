About Sheila

A lifelong interest in design, developed into practical web expertise

I have been interested in design since childhood—first through colour, lettering, illustration, and composition, and later through the way a well-designed website can make information feel clear and inviting.

That early curiosity became a keen, long-lasting interest in the field. I graduated from Academy of Learning Career College, completing design coursework delivered through Sessions College and building skills in visual communication, digital design, and website creation.

Graduate Design coursework delivered through Sessions College
Since 1997

A design education shaped for the digital world

Founded in New York City in 1997, Sessions College was an early specialist in online design and digital-media education. Its project-based tradition brings design, technology, communication, and creative problem-solving together—an approach that continues to inform how I work.

Today, I combine that foundation with responsive web design, careful planning, and clear one-to-one communication. My goal is to create a website that suits your business, supports your audience, and feels genuinely connected to what you do.
