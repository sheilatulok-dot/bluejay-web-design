New Case Study

Dove Farms Winery

A vintage student winery concept rebuilt as a refined, responsive hospitality website.

Responsive winery concept
dove farms portfolio concept
College concept Independent rebuild Hospitality

A heritage winery story reorganized for a modern visitor experience

The original Dove Farms website was created through Sessions College coursework. I rebuilt the fictional winery concept with a stronger visual hierarchy, responsive navigation, an editorial wine collection, clearer visit information, and purposeful interactions including animated navigation, image-detail reveals, progressive content motion, and an integrated Paso Robles regional map.

Original identity retained

The two-dove logo and central family-winery idea remain part of the project history, while the new palette, photography, typography, and layout give the concept a more established hospitality character.

Project type
Educational concept, independently rebuilt
My role
Visual redesign, content structure, interaction design, responsive development
Tools
HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, AI-assisted art direction
